Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

📍 Location Data

Your GPS location is processed entirely on your device. We do not store, transmit, or log your location to any server. Ever.

🔒 No Account Required

Flock Alert does not require you to create an account. All core features work without providing any personal information.

📷 Camera Reports

When you voluntarily submit a camera report, we collect only the camera location, type, and optional photo. No personal identifying information is attached to reports.

📊 Analytics

We collect only anonymous crash reports to improve app stability. No usage analytics, behavioral tracking, or ad targeting of any kind.

💳 Subscriptions

Subscription management is handled entirely by Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We never see or store your payment information.

🚫 Data Sales

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes. Period.
